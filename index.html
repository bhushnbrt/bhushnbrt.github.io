Hello, World! I am a second-year Ph.D. student in machine learning at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI), where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Kun Zhang. I am a member of the Center for Integrative Artificial Intelligence (CIAI), led by MBZUAI president Prof. Eric Xing and center director Prof. Kun Zhang. My research interest lies at the intersection of machine learning and causality.
